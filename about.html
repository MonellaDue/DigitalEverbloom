ABOUT US

Hi! This is Mae of Digital Everbloom, 27- year-old entrepreneur, former Recruitment and Sales Admin (Freelance), and a Digital Product Seller based in Pampanga.


I used to work in a corporate world where I experienced commuting regularly, leaving me no time for my family because the heavy traffic outside was getting the time that I could have been spending with my loved ones. So, I started to embrace the truth that we can go digital and explore the world online.


So here I am today, wanting to be of help to my co-parents or mommies out there so that they can keep their sons and daughters busy at home and mold them to be intelligent kids who are ready to see the world even when they are working and busy cleaning the house.


Keep on blooming everywhere.


God loves us!
